## üíé Design & Quality Principles

- **Human-First Development**: AI handles the boilerplate; humans focus on the architecture. Our design system prioritizes agent-driven assembly with human-level polish.
- **Sovereign Systems**: Backend complexity is a design flaw. Frontend should be smart enough to handle agentic requests with high predictability.
- **Performance by Default**: Senior-level engineering means zero-latency for interaction and minimal bundle overhead, even in agent-heavy workflows.
- **Tradeoffs Accepted**: Choosing Vue & React for different parts of the ecosystem to prove that senior engineers can build high-quality solutions regardless of the framework.
